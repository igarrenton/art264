la Catrina

A 3d character study inspired by the Mexican Dia de los Muertos

This animation is an exercise in character madeling and manipulation. The central figure was modeledafter anatomical studies and is fully controlled by an inverse kinetic rig.

The setting is designed after actual traditional Mexican Cemeteries. I studied layout and design of these places and pulled it into this piece for a more authentic feel

This was my first attempt at cloth simulation in a 3d environment, something I would like to attempt again.
