Blast Off

An animated informational site for children.

This is a one-page informative site for children. It gives information about the layers of the Earth's Atmosphere, and is designed for a school aged audience. The site loads on the bottom and as the user scrolls up the rocket moves through the atmospheric layers.

Animations are used throughout to draw interest. All the artwork for this page was painted by hand to give the site a warmer feeling to appeal to children.

Mood Boards

Two mood boards were designed for this project. The first was meant to convey a sense of fun and childishness, the second borrows from retro-styled space themed material. In the end, I chose to go with the retro theme, but saturate the colors and keep some fun/ friendly aspects of the first graphic style.

Style Tile & Navigation Scheme

This page uses saturated vibrant colors and high contrast to appeal to the young audience. I used hand painted illustrations and retro-style navigational buttons to keep with the chosen retro-space graphic style. Font were chosen that were rounded enough to be friendly to appeal to children while keeping within the chosen style.
