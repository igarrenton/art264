Antikythera

A working 3D model based on the Antikythera Mechanism

This animation was designed to have enough detail to be documentary in nature, while at the same time evocative of the mechanism ancient and mysterious nature.

A great deal of research was put into this so that the animated piece works in the same way as the the artifact is believed to have functioned. All gears turn off a central solar gear and work to approximate the position of the planets and other celestial cycles.
